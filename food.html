Food in Dubai

These are some of my favourite foods in Dubai!

Traditional Dubai Breakfast

A typical breakfast in Dubai includes baith tamat which is a delicious combination of scrambled eggs with tomatoes, herbs and luxurious saffron. Other essentials are balaleet, khameer and chebab which is served with a side of date syrup.

Sausages, bacon, fried eggs, black pudding, white pudding, baked beans, hash browns, mushrooms, grilled tomato and toast, with tea or coffee. Eggs Royale is a very traditional, classic breakfast dish.
